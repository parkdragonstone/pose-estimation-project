Core Research Pipeline

01
Data Acquisition
Synchronized multi-camera video, motion capture system, force plate, and IMU measurement.
02
2D Pose Estimation
YOLO + RTMPose for multi-view analysis and fine-tuned MMPose for single-view golf swing analysis.
03
3D Reconstruction
Triangulation in calibrated multi-view space, or SMPL + optimization for front-view single-camera input.
04
Signal Processing
Hampel outlier rejection and Butterworth low-pass filtering for stable marker trajectories.
05
Biomechanics
Marker augmentation, OpenSim inverse kinematics, joint angle time-series, and kinematic analysis.
